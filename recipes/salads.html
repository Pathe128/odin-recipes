Salads

Salads are versatile and refreshing dishes that typically consist of a combination of raw or cooked vegetables, fruits, proteins, grains, and dressings. They are known for their crisp textures, vibrant colors, and balanced flavors, making them popular choices for appetizers, side dishes, or even main courses. Salads can range from simple garden salads with lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, and a light vinaigrette to more complex creations like Caesar salads with grilled chicken, croutons, Parmesan cheese, and creamy dressing.

Greek Salad

Ingredients:

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Cucumbers
  • Tomatoes
  • Red onion
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta cheese
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces. Place it in a large salad bowl.
  2. Peel and dice the cucumbers. Add them to the salad bowl.
  3. Cut the tomatoes into wedges or chunks and add them to the bowl.
  4. Thinly slice the red onion and add it to the salad.
  5. Add Kalamata olives (whole or sliced) and crumbled feta cheese to the salad bowl.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together extra virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  7. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients evenly.
  8. Let the salad sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  9. Give the salad a final toss before serving.
  10. Enjoy your refreshing Greek salad as a side dish or a light meal!
